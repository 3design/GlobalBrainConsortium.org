Get Involved

The GBC is aligned with the neuroinformatics and computational research priorities of Healthy Brains for Healthy Lives (HBHL), in particular fostering the development of analytic frameworks to understand and predict individual differences in disease vulnerability and progression for those with depression, epilepsy, and sleep disorders.

If you are interested in learning more about the GBC, or would like to get involved, please contact us by filling out the following form.

    There are several ways to get involved in the GBC
  • Share your unique perspectives, experiences, and knowledge with members around the world to create solutions that positively impact global mental health.
  • Enhance your expertise by networking with leading professionals, discovering the latest trends in brain imaging, leveraging your scientific research, and being part of a global brain network.
  • Become a member of one of our working groups or create your own special interest group.
