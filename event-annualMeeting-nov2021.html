GBC Annual Meeting, November 22-23, 2021

The 2021 GBC Annual meeting will be held virtually on November 22 and 23. Delegates from around the world will attend, representing academic institutions, government and global organizations.

The aim this year is to organize the GBC into distinct projects leveraging collaborative neuroscience to increase access for LMIC to neuroelectrophysiology data access, standards, neuroinformatics methods and infrastructure, best practices for open science in the international arena, and potential applications for disease/disorder discovery or equitable global mental health.

The GBC Annual meeting will be followed by the Vera Gross public lecture delivered by Dr. Janina Galler on the Barbados Nutrition Study.

For more information, please contact info@globalbrainconsortium.org
