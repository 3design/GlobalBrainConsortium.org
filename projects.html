Global EEG Norms

Global EEG Norms

Creating international standards for M/EEG analysis

This project aims to create international standards for M/EEG analysis and method development, involving researchers spanning 9 countries around the world, led by Dr. Pedro Valdes-Sosa. Goals include:

  • Creating QEEG Signatures to describe electrophysiological activity
  • Data Standardization and Quality Control
  • Novel normative methodologies

Barbados Nutrition Study

Barbados Nutrition Study

Longitudinal multi-modal developmental cohort

Since the 1970s, this study led by Dr. Janina Galler [link] has led global efforts to assess the impact of nutrition in development.

  • Long-term clinical and demographic data collection
  • Social Outcomes
  • EEG data collection
