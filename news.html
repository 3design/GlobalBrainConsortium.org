2021 IOP conference

World Congress of Psychophysiology

iop2021.com
7-11 September 2021

GBC-related research activities presented at this event included:

  • Keynote by Alan Evans – Open Neuroscience
  • Keynote by Jorge Bosch – EEG based brain connectivity methods
  • Keynote by Paolo Rossini – Brain network Analysis via Graph Theory
  • Keynote by Soctt Makeig - Advancing high resolution brain-body imaging
  • Pedro Valdes-Sosa Symposium on Electrophysiology as a Tool for Global Health
  • Jorge Bosch Symposium on Brain Connectivity
  • Jorge Riera Symposium - Origins of the event-related EEG
  • Anne Gallagher & Sarah Lippe Symposium - Innovative Brain Recording Methods
  • Thalia Harmony Symposium - Cognitive Activity in Infants at Risk of Brain Damage
  • Fuleah Razzaq Symposium - Long-term malnutrition effects. The Barbados study
  • Armida Mucci & Claudio Babiloni Symposium - Do Mental Disorders Surf on EEG Waves

IFCN 2019

International Federation of Clinical Neurophysiology annual meeting

12 June 2019

Global Brain Consortium Symposium: Neural Basis of human consciousness: phenomena, paradigms, and exploring techniques. Presentations included:

  • Claudio Babiloni, Sapienza Univeristy of Rome: "Primary visual consciousness of qualita: the neural basis"
  • Aina Puce, Indiana U: "Consciousness and Social representation in the brain"
  • Chaired by Alan Evans
