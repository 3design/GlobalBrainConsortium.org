Cuba Workshop 2020 Summary

The second GBC meeting was held in Varadero, Cuba on Feb.28-March 1, 2020. 76 delegates from academic institutions, governmental and global organizations attended in-person, and sessions were hosted interactively online for remote participants and speakers. During the event, 9 working groups met to present updates and ongoing activities. Highlights include:

  • Research Priorities
  • Research Standards
  • Best Practices
